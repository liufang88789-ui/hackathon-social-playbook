# 4-Week Content Plan Template

## Week 1: Foundation
- Day 1: Project intro tweet + thread
- Day 2: Problem statement thread (why this matters)
- Day 3: Team introduction (who we are, why we're building this)
- Day 4: Technical approach thread (how we're solving it)
- Day 5: Community engagement (reply to 10 similar projects)
- Day 6: First demo screenshot/teaser
- Day 7: Week 1 recap + what's coming

## Week 2: Traction
- Daily devlog-style updates
- Engage with 5 similar projects daily
- Post 2 hot takes on ecosystem topics

## Week 3: Build
- Progress threads every other day
- Spaces participation if available
- User testing feedback posts

## Week 4: Final Push
- Final product thread
- Demo day countdown
- Result announcement + learnings

## Hashtag Strategy
Primary: #Colosseum #Solana #Hackathon
Secondary: Track-specific tags
